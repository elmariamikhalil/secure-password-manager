Thank you for installing SecureVault Password Manager! Here's how to get started.

Create Your Account

Start by creating a secure account with a strong master password. This password will be used to encrypt all your data and cannot be recovered if forgotten.

Remember: Your master password is the key to all your passwords. Make it strong but memorable!

Add Your Passwords

After setting up your account, start adding your existing passwords to your vault. You can import passwords from your browser or add them manually.

For each website you visit, SecureVault can also offer to save new passwords automatically.

Use Auto-Fill

When you visit a website with saved credentials, SecureVault will offer to automatically fill in your username and password.

Just click on the SecureVault icon in the login field or use the extension popup.

Access Anywhere

Your encrypted passwords sync securely across your devices. Use our web vault or mobile apps to access your passwords from anywhere.
